Style Guide

Purpose/Theme

The purpose of our site is to learn the beauty of CSS while following the Zen Garden rules of design. We will familiarize ourselves with SCSS and @imports along with many new concepts. Our goal is to put into action the items we are learning during this course. The theme of our site is “On the Road to Enlightenment”. We will use a street and road travel as our theme. We plan on having a background that gives the illusion of a road, and our street sign will be a part of the navigation on the site. Our links will be specialized with some of them lighting up when you hover and hopefully reminding viewers of headlights and traffic lights. Our theme will be subtle and show off the new skills we have acquired. Our site will present a unique perspective on the road to enlightenment.
